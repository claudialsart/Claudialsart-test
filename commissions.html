Commissions

I work with collectors to create paintings that carry a personal meaning. Together we define palette, mood, and symbolism, shaping a piece that feels both intimate and timeless.

The commission process

Commissioning a painting is a simple, collaborative process. It starts with a short conversation about what you have in mind, where the piece will live, and how you want it to feel. You do not need a perfect idea. I can guide you with suggestions on size, composition, and colour palette.

Once we agree on the concept, size, and price, a deposit secures your spot in my schedule. During the painting process I may share progress updates, especially if the piece is very personal, so you can feel involved without needing to direct every detail.

When the painting is finished, I will send final photos for approval, then carefully pack it and ship it to you. Each work is painted in professional oil colours on quality surfaces and finished with a protective varnish once fully dry.

Pricing guide

Prices depend on size, level of detail, and the complexity of the idea. To give you a rough guide, most commissions fall within these ranges:

  • Small works up to 30 × 40 cm from £300
  • Medium works around 40 × 50 cm to 50 × 70 cm from £550
  • Larger pieces from 60 × 80 cm and above from £900

These are starting points. After you send your enquiry, I will reply with a clear quote based on your idea and chosen size. A deposit, usually around 30 percent, is required to begin and is non-refundable once I start the painting.
