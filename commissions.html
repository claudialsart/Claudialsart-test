Commissions

I work with collectors to create paintings that hold personal meaning. Together we define palette, mood, and symbolism, shaping a piece that feels both intimate and timeless.

The commission process

Commissioning a painting is simple and collaborative. It begins with a short conversation about your idea — where the piece will live, the feeling you want it to carry, and any palette or symbolism that matters to you. You don’t need a perfect vision. I can guide you with suggestions for size, composition, and colour.

Once we agree on the concept, size, and price, a deposit secures your place in my schedule. During the creation process, I may share progress updates, especially for personal pieces, so you can feel involved while still giving the work space to grow naturally.

When the painting is complete, I’ll send final photos for approval, then safely package and ship it to you. All works are painted in professional oil colours on archival surfaces and varnished once fully cured.

Pricing guide

Prices vary depending on size, detail, and complexity. Most commissions usually fall within these ranges:

  • Small works up to 30 × 40 cm — from £300
  • Medium works around 40 × 50 cm to 50 × 70 cm — from £550
  • Large works from 60 × 80 cm and above — from £900

These are starting points. After you send your enquiry, I’ll reply with a clear, personalised quote based on your idea. A deposit (around 30 percent) is required to begin and becomes non-refundable once work starts.
